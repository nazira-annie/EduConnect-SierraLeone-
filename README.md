# EduConnect-SierraLeone-
Responsive landing page for EduConnect Sierra Leone — a Digital Public Good promoting quality education
DPG Idea Developmen

* Problem Statement: 
  Sierra Leone faces challenges with equitable access to quality education, especially in rural areas where resources, trained teachers, and digital learning tools are limited. Many students lack access to updated learning materials, and teachers struggle with professional development opportunities.  

Target Users
  -Primary & secondary school students in rural and urban Sierra Leone  
 -Teachers seeking digital resources and training  
 -Education policymakers and NGOs monitoring progress  

SDG Alignment
  SDG 4: Quality Education– Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities.  
  SDG 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure– Build resilient infrastructure and foster innovation through digital platforms.  
  SDG 10: Reduced Inequalities– Bridge the gap between rural and urban education access.
